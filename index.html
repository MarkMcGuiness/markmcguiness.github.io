Welcome to my Portfolio

 When I was seven years old, I drew a picture of myself in crayon, with the caption, 'When I grow up, I want to be a mad scientist, so I can learn how to make things.'

The Crayon scratching, turned to pencil sketching, then to songwriting, and now computer graphics and web development. I may not be making literal Time Machines like I wanted when I was seven, but I always strive to make and create and come up with ideas, and I always search for new ways to make those ideas into reality.

I made this website to showcase my work across various mediums. The website itself I hand coded using native HTML and CSS using Bootstrap. I am certified in Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator and am proficient in most of the Adobe creative suite. I also have training in using Blender for 3D modeling and animation. I have basic knowledge of using wordpress.org and XAMPP, as well as utilizing Google Analytics. I also play the Piano and spent many years with a band, writing, recording, performing, promoting, and merchandising. Thank you for visiting my website, and enjoy!
